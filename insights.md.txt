1. Monthly Revenue Trends
Revenue shows clear fluctuations month to month, suggesting seasonal purchasing patterns. Understanding these trends can help businesses better forecast demand and plan marketing campaigns.

2. Geographic Trends
Revenue varies significantly by state, indicating stronger demand in certain regions.

3. Top Revenue-Generating Product Categories
A small number of product categories contribute a large share of total revenue, indicating that product demand is concentrated among a few key categories.

4. Customer Purchase Behavior
Customer purchase patterns show that many customers place only a small number of orders, while a smaller group of "High-value" repeat customers contributes significantly to total sales.

5. Delivery Performance
Late deliveries are associated with lower customer review scores.

6. Business Implication
Focusing marketing efforts on high-performing product categories and encouraging repeat purchases could significantly increase overall revenue.
